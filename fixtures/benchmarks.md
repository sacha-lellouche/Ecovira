# Ecovira Benchmark Data

Industry benchmarks for 4-star independent hotels (25-80 rooms).

## Energy Consumption

- **Benchmark**: 3,500 kWh/room/year (291.7 kWh/room/month)
- **Source**: European Hotel Energy Benchmarking Tool (EHEN), France segment
- **Range**: 
  - Efficient: < 3,000 kWh/room/year
  - Average: 3,000-4,000 kWh/room/year
  - High: > 4,000 kWh/room/year

## Water Consumption

- **Benchmark**: 120 m³/room/year (10 m³/room/month)
- **Source**: Green Key France, 4-star category
- **Range**:
  - Efficient: < 100 m³/room/year
  - Average: 100-150 m³/room/year
  - High: > 150 m³/room/year

## Waste Generation

- **Benchmark**: 350 kg/room/year (29.2 kg/room/month)
- **Source**: ADEME (French Environment Agency)
- **Range**:
  - Efficient: < 300 kg/room/year
  - Average: 300-400 kg/room/year
  - High: > 400 kg/room/year

## CO2 Emissions

- **France electricity grid factor**: 0.233 kg CO2e/kWh (2023)
- **Water treatment factor**: 0.5 kg CO2e/m³ (estimate)
- **Benchmark total**: 70-85 kg CO2e/room/month

## Occupancy Assumptions

- **Average 4-star occupancy (France)**: 70-75%
- **Average guests per room**: 1.8
- **Guest nights per room per month**: ~22 (at 72% occupancy)

## Certification Thresholds

### Green Key France
- Energy: < 50 kWh/guest night
- Water: < 0.4 m³/guest night
- Waste recycling: > 30%
- Renewable energy: > 10% of total

### Green Globe
- Energy reduction: -5% year-over-year improvement target
- Water reduction: -5% year-over-year improvement target
- Carbon footprint: Documented and offset plan required

## Notes

- These benchmarks are for **mainland France** climate zones
- Adjust for hotel-specific factors: spa, restaurant size, pool, laundry on-site
- Seasonal variation expected: ±15% summer/winter for energy
